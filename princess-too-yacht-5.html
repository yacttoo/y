About Princess Too

Princess Too is a magnificent 47.55-meter luxury motor yacht built by the prestigious Dutch shipyard Feadship in 1999. This exceptional vessel represents the pinnacle of yacht craftsmanship, featuring exterior design by the renowned De Voogt Naval Architects and interior design by the acclaimed Sam Sorgiovanni.

Originally known as Excellence II, Princess Too underwent significant refits in 2006, 2014, and 2017, ensuring she maintains the highest standards of luxury and performance. With her steel hull and aluminum superstructure, she offers both elegance and durability for the most discerning yacht enthusiasts.

The yacht can accommodate up to 12 guests in 6 luxurious suites, including a master stateroom with Jacuzzi, one VIP cabin, and four twin guest cabins. A professional crew of 10 ensures an unparalleled level of service throughout your voyage.
